The Coordinate System

The 2D context provided by the canvas has a coordinate system consisting of X and Y values.

Starting at the top, left of the canvas is the point (0, 0).

As you move down the canvas Y gets larger.

As you move right on the canvas X gets larger.

So the point (100, 50) means you move right 100 and down 50.

Explore The Coordinate System.

Mouse over the grey area to the right to see where your mouse is in the coordinate system.
